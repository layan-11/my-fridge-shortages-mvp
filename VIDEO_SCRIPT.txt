My Fridge Shortages — Demo Script (5 minutes)

1) Intro (20s)
- "Hi, I'm [Name]. This is My Fridge Shortages — a prototype that helps detect missing items in your fridge and generate a smart shopping list."

2) Project purpose (30s)
- Explain the problem (duplicate buys, forgetting items, food waste).
- Say what the system does: captures fridge contents, detects shortages, creates shopping list.

3) Show UI (30s)
- Open index.html and show the Login screen.
- Click Login (mock) to go to Home.

4) Home / Inventory (60s)
- Explain the inventory cards: name, quantity, expiry.
- Point out 'Out of stock' badge for Cheese.

5) Shortages (60s)
- Click Shortages page — it shows items missing from essential list (e.g., Cheese, Bread, Yogurt).
- Click "Add to shopping" to move items to shopping list.

6) Shopping List (40s)
- Show the items added.
- Demonstrate removing an item / clearing list.

7) Technical notes (40s)
- Mention mock data and where detection logic is (app.js).
- Explain how real system would connect camera + TensorFlow + backend (Node.js + Firebase).

8) Wrap-up (20s)
- Summarize benefits and next steps.
- "Thank you" and invite questions.

Total: ~4-5 minutes.
